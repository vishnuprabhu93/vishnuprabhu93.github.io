The demo below is a conceptual, **synthetic-data** illustration of the closed adaptive loop: it
*senses* physiological signals, *infers* an arousal state via a simple inspectable rule, *adapts*
a stylized intervention, and re-senses — continuously. Inject a stress event and toggle
**Adaptive ON / OFF** to watch the loop either regulate back to baseline or let the perturbation
linger. Everything is generated in your browser; no real sensor or patient data is used.
