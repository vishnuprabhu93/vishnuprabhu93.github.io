Dr. Prabhu co-hosted the NSF-funded AI, Robotics, and Industry 5.0 Summer Camp at NeoCity Academy, held July
6–9, 2026. Dr. Prabhu served as Co-Principal Investigator alongside Principal Investigator Dr. Arthur Huang and
Co-Principal Investigator Dr. Mehmet Altin.
