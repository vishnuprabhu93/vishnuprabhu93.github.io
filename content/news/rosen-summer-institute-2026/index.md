Dr. Prabhu co-hosted the NSF- and VentureWell-funded AI, Robotics, and Entrepreneurship Summer Institute, held
June 8–12, 2026 at the Rosen College of Hospitality Management. Dr. Prabhu served as Co-Principal Investigator
alongside Principal Investigator Dr. Arthur Huang and Co-Principal Investigator Dr. Mehmet Altin.
